Our Story

How it all started basically

The beginning

Started small

Back in 2016, two friends just decided to open a small cafe. Nothing too big, just a simple place with coffee and some seating.

Over time more people started coming regularly and it slowly grew. We still try to keep things simple and not overcomplicate stuff.
