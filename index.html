Why visit us

More than just coffee

Single Origin Beans

We source our beans directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Every batch is freshly roasted in small quantities so you always get peak flavour.

Baked Fresh Daily

Our kitchen starts before sunrise. Croissants, sourdough loaves, muffins, and seasonal pastries come straight from the oven every single morning.

Locally Sourced

We partner with nearby farms for our dairy, eggs, and produce. Seasonal ingredients shape our menu, which means it changes often and stays honest.
